Our Story

Partner Attribution AI was born out of a simple frustration: Why is tracking partner contributions still so manual?

Our founders spent years running partner programs at fast-growing SaaS companies. Every month, they'd spend countless hours in spreadsheets trying to figure out which partners deserved credit for which deals. Partners would fight over attribution. Commissions would be wrong. Trust eroded.

When AI became powerful enough to understand complex customer journeys, we saw an opportunity. What if attribution could be automatic? What if it could be fair? What if partners could see their impact in real-time?
