# Covant — Discovery Target List

*50 B2B SaaS companies running channel / partner programs that fit our ICP. Ranked into three tiers by problem severity × accessibility × design-partner value.*

---

## How to read this doc

**Confidence flags** (column: **Conf**):
- 🟢 Confirmed — public partner directory or PRM listing I'm confident exists today.
- 🟡 Likely — known to run a partner motion but I haven't verified the specific surface (portal, PRM, HoP) in the last 90 days.
- 🔴 Best guess — plausibly fits the ICP; verify before sending.

**Buyer column:** I do not name specific people. Titles change fast and I refuse to hallucinate names. Before sending, look up the Head of Partnerships / Head of Alliances / Director of Channel on LinkedIn. If the role is empty, go one level up (VP RevOps, CRO) or pass.

**ARR bands** are estimates from public fundraising + headcount signals. Treat them as rough.

**Before sending to any row on this list:**
1. Confirm on LinkedIn that the buyer title is filled.
2. Confirm on the company site that the partner program still exists.
3. Rewrite the **Outreach angle** column as the email's first line — it's the hook.

## Verification checklist before you hit send on any row

- [ ] LinkedIn: Head of Partnerships (or equivalent) currently in role at the company.
- [ ] Company site: partner portal / partner page still live.
- [ ] G2 or Reddit: any public complaint about current PRM (bonus signal).
- [ ] Outreach angle column rewritten as *their* specific hook — not the generic one I drafted.
- [ ] Warm path noted if a 2nd-degree LinkedIn connection exists.

## If the response rate is below 10% after 30 sent

The hook is generic. Rewrite Line 1 of every email to reference a specific artifact on their partner page — a named tier, a named perk, a partner directory count. Generic hooks lose before the email is opened.
